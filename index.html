About the Book

The awakening starts within.

Awakened by Genetics is a powerful exploration of identity, heritage, and spiritual awakening. Lawrence Reagor shares a deeply personal journey from a challenging upbringing in Vallejo, California, through experiences shaped by street life, music, family struggles, faith, and self-discovery.

The book reflects on hardship, mentors, family, and the pursuit of truth through the church and music. Lawrence recounts rigorous study of Scripture and his continuing journey to learn Biblical Hebrew to deepen his understanding of biblical history.

Part autobiography and part guide to self-awareness, the book invites readers to reflect on faith, resilience, identity, history, spirituality, and the power of personal transformation.
